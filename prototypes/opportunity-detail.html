Description

The XRPL Grant Program Q1 2024 is seeking talented developers to create innovative DeFi applications that leverage the unique capabilities of the XRP Ledger, including fast transactions, low cost, and energy sustainability.

Focus Areas:

  • AMMs (Automated Market Makers) and Liquidity Pools
  • Lending and Borrowing Protocols
  • Stablecoin Infrastructure
  • Cross-chain Bridges
  • Yield Farming Platforms

Grants range from $25K to $100K depending on project scope. Includes technical mentorship from the Ripple team and access to exclusive XRPL community resources.
